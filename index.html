Who We Are

Built on
Integrity
& Expertise

Gee M Projects (PTY) LTD is a specialised consulting engineering company offering integrated engineering and project management services to the commercial, industrial, municipal, and governmental sectors.

Since its inception, Gee M Projects has built a solid reputation and served as a role model to other black-owned companies when it comes to excellence in professional service delivery, best practice in corporate governance, and business ethics.

The firm is run by directors who are professional engineers directly involved in the day-to-day running of the business. All projects are carried out under the strict supervision of registered professional engineers.
